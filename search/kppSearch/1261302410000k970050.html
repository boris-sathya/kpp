Welcome to Building Markets

The first and only online marketplace for Afghanistan.

Building Markets is an online resource for Buyers and Suppliers operating in Afghanistan.

Looking for goods and services in Afghanistan?  Looking for sub contractors?

The PDT supplier directory lists over 4,000 businesses. Most of these businesses are located in Balkh, Kabul, Kandahar, Helmand, Herat, and Nangarhar Provinces.

The most represented sectors are: Agriculture and Agricultural Related Products and Services, Associations and Unions, Automotive, Business and Consulting Services, Construction and Renovation, Financial and Legal Services, Food and Beverages, Furniture and Office Machines, Generators, Heavy Machinery and Solar Panels, Handicrafts, Health and Medicine, Home Essentials, Information Technologies Services and Equipment, Laundry, Cleaning Services and Waste Collection, Media and Communication, Oil, Coal, Fuels and Natural Gas, Printing and Copying, Real Estate and Insurance, Restaurants and Catering, Security Services and Equipment, Telecommunication Services and Equipment, Transportation, Logistics and Courier Services, Travel and Lodging.
