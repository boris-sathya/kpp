Tech Reunions 2010

Mark your calendars! Tech Reunions is the largest annual gathering of MIT alumni on campus. Each class celebrates its reunion every five years- from the 5th to the 70th. Regardless of your year or degree, Tech Reunions features a not-to-be missed mix of class-specific events as well as festivities for all MIT alumni and graduate school alumni.
