Linux User Groups

The following list of user groups are organized by country. Some hold monthly or quarterly meetings, while others maintain active mailing lists and newsletters.

If your group is not included in our list, please email webmaster@linux.org with all the details. There's no charge to be included on our site, and we'll automatically create a link to your group's web site as long as you give us the URL!
