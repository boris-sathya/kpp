Donations

Linux is built by a community of developers, documenters, consultants, service technicians and users, each making their own special contribution. This site is a showcase for our collective work and is built to inform and celebrate the system that is Linux.

Making a donation to this site is your opportunity to help build Linux Online and give something back to the community that has brought Linux to the forefront of 21st century computing!

Check out our exciting gifts below. Get some neat Linux Online items as our thanks for your much needed contribution.

Methods

We have a number of different ways you can make a donation to the site. Currently, you can:

  • Login to your PayPal account and use "Send Money" to us at: admin (at) linux.org.
  • Go to our PayPal donation page:  .
  • Send a fax to +1-315-393-1154 with:

    • "I would like to make a donation to Linux Online."
    • Your name
    • Billing address (street, city, state, country, zip/code)
    • Telephone number
    • Email address
    • Visa, Mastercard, American Express or Discover card number & expiration date
    • Amount of the donation
    • Your signature
    • Your gift choice (if applicable)

    Coming soon is an online form to submit your card information!

  • Mail a US dollar cheque or money order to us at:

    Ogdensburg, NY 13669
    USA
    Please DO NOT SEND CASH in the mail!

Please be sure to send us your address and your gift choice (if applicable).

Each donation we receive, large or small, makes an important contribution to maintaining this site!
