Vendors

We have classified our vendors based on the products they sell. Where necessary, each of the following categories has been further broken down into subcategories:


  • Consulting
  • Hardware
  • Internet
  • Media
  • Retailers
  • Software
  • Support
  • Systems
  • Training

Categories
  • Consulting (112)
    Companies that provide consulting services related to the deployment of Linux in businesses. They can provide you with assessment on how Linux can offer you a solution for the computing needs of your company or organization.

  • Hardware (4)
    Companies that manufacture and sell hardware and peripherals that are Linux-friendly. If you're looking for drivers or need to know if your hardware is supported, this is a good place to find out.

    • Automation (10)
      Companies that sell internal hardware and devices that work in automation of industrial processes

    • Controller Cards (10)
      Manufacturers of cards that control internal and external devices

    • Internet (9)
      Manufacturers of routers and other remote connectivity devices that use or integrate with Linux

    • Memory (7)
      Linux friendly memory vendors

    • Motherboards (6)
      Manufacturers of motherboards/mainboards that run under Linux

    • Multimedia (7)
      Manufacturers of sound, video, radio, tv and other cards that work with Linux.

    • Network (28)
      Manufacturers of network cards that run under Linux

    • Peripherals (11)
      Manufacturers of mice, keyboards, cameras and other plugin devices with Linux support

    • SingleBoard (2)
      Manufacturers of single board computers (SMB) that run embedded Linux

    • Storage (24)
      Manufacturers of Linux-friendly hard disks, raid arrays, CD, DVD and backup devices.

    • Telephony (8)
      Companies that manufacture telephony hardware supporting Linux

    • Wireless (6)
      Companies that manufacture and support hareware to be used in wireless computing

  • Internet (10)
    Companies that provide Internet connection, broadband services and hosting that's Linux-friendly. Companies that use Linux-powered servers to provide their services also appear in this list.

    • Access (ISP) (2)
      Companies that will provide you with dial-up and broadband Internet access that's Linux-friendly

    • Hosting (20)
      Companies that host internet pages on servers running Linux

  • Media (1)
    Media companies offering or specializing in Linux information

    • Magazines (1)
      Print magazines dedicated to Linux

    • Websites (10)
      Websites offering Linux news and information

  • Retailers (78)
    Retail sellers of Linux distributions, software on CDs and other merchandise related to Linux, like t-shirts, posters and toys.

    • Applications (18)
      A list of resellers of software that runs with Linux.

    • Books (15)
      Publishers of Linux related books

    • Distributions (66)
      Resellers of Linux distributions.

    • Novelty items (12)
      Companies that sell items for Linux enthusiasts like posters, mugs, t-shirts and toys.

  • Software (161)
    Companies that produce software that's designed to run with Linux. If you are looking for a specific application, check out our applications section.

  • Support (97)
    Companies that provide technical support for any type of system running Linux in any environment.

  • Systems (102)
    Companies that sell Linux pre-installed on computer systems ranging from laptops to desktop models to servers and clusters.

    • Appliances (28)
      Companies that sell items that use Linux in black box or real time environments. For routers, bridges, etc check the Hardware category.

    • Clusters (12)
      Companies that will sell and set up and/or maintain a Linux cluster for use in high-powered computing enviroments.

    • Desktops (52)
      Companies that sell standard desktop and tower model computers with Linux pre-installed.

    • Hand-helds (4)
      Here you'll find a list of both resellers and manufacturers of hand-held computers and personal digital assistants (PDAs).

    • Laptops (24)
      Companies that sell laptop/portable computers with Linux installed.

    • Servers (82)
      Companies that sell servers powered by Linux to businesses of all sizes.

  • Training (5)
    Information about companies that provide classes and courses on how to use Linux in all of its possible types of deployment and well as organizations that provide and/or prepare for Linux certification. Here you'll find organizations that help you prepare and/or offer professional certificates to accredit your knowledge of Linux operating system configuration and administration.

    • Administrator (37)
      Companies and organizations that will teach the you administer multiple Linux systems.

    • Certification (9)
      Here is a list of companies that issue Linux certification and prepare for the exams to receive these certificates.

    • Preparatory (13)
      Here is a list of companies that help candidates prepare for Linux certification exams.

    • User (26)
      Companies and organizations that will teach the you how to use Linux in a home or office setting.
