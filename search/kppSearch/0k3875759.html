User Area

Linux is built upon the contribution of 1000s of developers worldwide and Linux Online is no different. We count on people like you to bring us new and interesting information, provide updates for our various databases and make suggestions on improving our site and our support of the Linux Operating System.

Registering at Linux Online is a great way for you to help improve this site. Each time you submit information on the site and we approve it for posting, you will earn TuxPointstm. Each month the user who earns the most TuxPoints will receive a certificate from Linux Online and a $20 gift certificate from Amazon.com or Linux Online. You will also be able to trade your TuxPoints for exclusive Linux Online merchandise at our online store.

Registration is required if you wish to participate in the following activities:

  • Post messages in online forum(s).
  • Purchase merchandise in our online store.
  • Enter giveaways.
  • Use URLWatch® to receive notices when we update information on the site.
  • Review changes submitted for a vendor, application, user group, distribution, project, etc that you created or submitted.
  • Receive the Linux Online Updatetm, our website newsletter.
  • Receive product announcements from Linux vendors.
  • View advertising statistics and reports online.
  • Become a subscriber of Linux Online.

A large number of people have asked us how they can contribute financially to this site. In order to give people this opportunity, we will soon be accepting subscriptions. Subscribers will receive a thank you gift as well as enjoy a discount on all purchases from our online store. Five different levels of subscription are available, each with different benefits.
